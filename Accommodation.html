Accommodation

Registration fees with accommodation at the Conference Hotel

(Miracle Hotel – http://www.miracleotel.com/)

Registration fee includes the following services:

  • Attendance to all sessions of both IMCW2014 and ICKM2014 conferences including the Abstracts book and the conference kit,
  • Opening cocktail; and Gala dinner.

Registration fees without accommodation

Registration fee includes the following services:

  • Lunches during the conference days (November 24-26, 2014),
  • Twice-a-day coffee breaks with cookies; and Opening cocktail.

Note: A separate fee (50 EUR) applies for Gala dinner for registrants not staying at the conference hotel (optional).
