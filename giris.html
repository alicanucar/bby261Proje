Early bird registration for IMCW2014 and ICKM2014 extended to August 11, 2014

        

Posted on July 31, 2014

        

The 5th International Symposium on Information Management in a Changing World (http://imcw2014.bilgiyonetimi.net/) and the 10th International Conference on Knowledge Management (http://ickm2014.bilgiyonetimi.net/) will take place in Antalya, Turkey, from November 24-26, 2014, and their preliminary programs are available online.

Due to a number of requests from would-be participants who are on summer vacation. More information about the registration fees is available at http://imcw2014.bilgiyonetimi.net/.
